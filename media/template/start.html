Privacy

Your genome will not be sent to any server, it remains on your computer. This website will make requests to a database that only contain "rsid" and "population" information. At no point will any genotypes be sent across the wires (all computation will be done in the browser).


Some exercises may request you to enter personal information, including genotypes or results of analyses. Entry of your data is not required and all information will be stored on a secure server and deleted in a timely fashion. However, there is a theoretical possibility that this information can be intercepted and misused.
