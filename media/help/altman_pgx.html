Pharmacogenomics SNPs


As with diseases and traits, genetic variants can influence an individuals response to various drugs. Many of the canonical examples are available at PharmGKB.

In this exercise, we will explore our variants at a few sites with known pharmacogenomic influences. As with all of these exercises, this information is for education and research purposes only. Individuals taking medications should not discontinue their use on the basis of these results: consult your physician with any concerns.
