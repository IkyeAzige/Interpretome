About Height Prediction

Height is a classic example of a "missing heritability" trait: it is known to be associated with genetic factors (approximately 75% heritable), but thus far, association studies have only been able to explain ~20% of the heritability.

In this exercise, we will attempt to predict your height based on 145 SNPs and compare this to a similar prediction based on the height of your parents.
